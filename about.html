Our Story

Founded in Addis Ababa, AxumiteEthiopiaTours was born from a simple belief: Ethiopia's extraordinary heritage, landscapes, and cultures deserve to be shared with the world — authentically and responsibly.

Our team of passionate local experts has spent over a decade crafting journeys that go beyond sightseeing. We connect travelers with the heartbeat of Ethiopia — from the ancient rock churches of Lalibela to the vibrant traditions of the Omo Valley.

Every tour is designed to leave a positive impact on the communities we visit, ensuring that tourism becomes a force for preservation and empowerment.
