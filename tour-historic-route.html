Journey Through Ethiopia's Sacred Heart

Experience Ethiopia's most sacred journey through the legendary Historic Route. This comprehensive 8-day adventure takes you deep into the heart of Ethiopia's Christian heritage, where ancient traditions and extraordinary architecture tell stories of faith that have endured for over a millennium.

Visit Lalibela's extraordinary rock-hewn churches - 11 monolithic masterpieces carved from single stone blocks in the 12th century. Marvel at the famous cross-shaped Bete Giyorgis, carved from the ground down in the shape of a perfect cross. Explore the interconnected network of underground tunnels that link these sacred spaces, creating a subterranean city of prayer and pilgrimage.

Travel to Axum, the ancient capital of the powerful Aksumite Empire (400 BCE - 1000 CE). Stand in awe before towering granite stelae reaching 24 meters high, marking royal tombs and demonstrating engineering sophistication that rivals ancient Egypt. Visit the Church of St. Mary of Zion, Ethiopia's holiest shrine believed to house the Ark of the Covenant. Explore the Queen of Sheba's palace ruins and discover why this UNESCO World Heritage site was once one of the world's great civilizations.
