Tiya Archaeological Expedition

Uncover Ethiopia's archaeological mysteries on this 1-day expedition to the Tiya World Heritage site. Walk among over 40 ancient carved stone stelae, some reaching 3 meters high, dating from the 12th-14th centuries. These mysterious monuments mark elite burial grounds and feature enigmatic symbols and human representations that continue to puzzle archaeologists. Gain fascinating insight into pre-Christian Ethiopian culture and ponder the meaning of these ancient stone markers in this important archaeological treasure.
